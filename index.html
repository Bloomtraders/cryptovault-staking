How does the Flexible Pool work?›
14% APY, no lock. Deposit from $5, withdraw anytime with no penalties. Daily compound at 0.038%/day.
How do I connect Trust Wallet?›
Click "Trust Wallet" on the login screen. If on mobile, it will open the Trust Wallet app automatically. On desktop, use WalletConnect to scan the QR code with Trust Wallet.
How long do deposits take?›
5–15 minutes after network confirmation for both BEP20 and TRC20.
Can I withdraw from a fixed pool early?›
Yes, with a 5% early exit fee. We recommend the Flexible Pool if you need liquidity.
Which networks are supported?›
BEP20 (Binance Smart Chain) and TRC20 (TRON Network). Always verify before sending.
